The philosophy of slow luxury drapes.

Everyday Ease

Breathable Flax Linen

Our linen fibers are sourced directly from the flax fields of Belgium, woven into structured but breathable sheets, and pre-washed with bio-enzymes to give them an immediate soft, lived-in feel. Each silhouette is designed with loose drops, minimal clean seams, and soft tones to accompany you through hot days and quiet weekends.

Generational Craft

Handloomed Mulberry Silk

We honor the handloom. Our silk sarees and festive jackets are woven in small batches in Varanasi and Maheshwar, where master craftsmen spend weeks detailing a single piece. By combining pure mulberry silk threads with antique gold zari lines, we create festive garments that hold the weight of heritage but remain light, liquid, and graceful on the body.
